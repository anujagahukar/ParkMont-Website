About us

ParkMont IT & Infra Pvt. Ltd., formerly known as ZPark Technologies Pvt. Ltd., was founded in 2022 with a clear vision: to modernize and revolutionize the parking infrastructure in India. Recognizing the growing need for efficient and smart parking solutions, we set out to bridge the gap between traditional parking systems and the demands of modern urban spaces. At ParkMont, we specialize in cutting-edge parking solutions that leverage the latest in technology. Our comprehensive systems integrate sensors, cameras, mobile apps, and cloud-based software to streamline the parking experience for both operators and users. By optimizing parking capacity and enhancing user convenience, we aim to reduce the stress and inefficiency often associated with parking in busy urban areas. Our commitment to innovation and excellence drives us to continuously improve and adapt our solutions, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of the industry. Whether you're managing a small parking lot or a large multi-level facility, ParkMont has the tools and expertise to help you maximize efficiency and customer satisfaction. Join us on our journey to transform parking infrastructure across India and beyond. At ParkMont, we're not just changing the way people park; we're creating smarter, more connected cities.
